Join the Wezesha Circle

In Swahili, “wezesha” means “to empower” or “enable” and it’s what we believe in. You, too, can join this life-giving work by empowering us to effectively assist those who need it most. By joining the Wezesha Circle, you can commit to monthly giving that will tremendously enhance the work that we’re able to do. Your support will impact generations of people.
