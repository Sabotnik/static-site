What We Do

Empower Tanzania works with many different groups and organizations on projects in Tanzania. We partner with rural communities and other organziations to measurably and sustainably improve the lives of Tanzanians.

Culture

We promote a culture of excellence, diversity, openness, integrity, and innovation within our board, staff, and partners.

Resources

We manage our resources with fiscal responsibility and efficiency to ensure maximum impact on investment with a minimum of overheads. We keep our overhead at 15% (or less), allowing the vast majority of funds to be used on projects.

Innovative Solutions

Our programs strive to provide sustainable, resilient, and innovative solutions for rural communities through a participatory and holistic approach.

Empowerment

We actively seek to build local capacity in Tanzania and empower rural Tanzanian communities, including previously disadvantaged groups.
