Protecting the Vulnerable from Covid-19

The people we serve in rural Tanzania already live without a safety net or access to adequate healthcare. Throughout the entire country of 60 million people there are less than 50 ICU beds and less than 30 ventilators. In the Kilimanjaro Region, where we serve, there is a total of 14 ICU beds and 4 ventilators.

The warnings from experts about the possible devastation throughout Africa has cemented our belief that it’s more important than ever to care of one another. We all share the same world, and we’re all in this together.

Our team in Tanzania is moving forward with optimism and trust. We are determined to help. If you are able, please join us as we jump to action.
