### Ingredients
* 1 head cauliflower
* Olive oil
* Red pepper flakes
* Ground pepper


### Procedure:
1. Chop cauliflower into bite size florets.
2. Place cauliflower in bowl or zip lock bag coat with olive
oil and seasonings.
